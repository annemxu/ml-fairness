Intro

ML tools are increasingly used for decision-making in contexts that affect everyday people, but may have lots of bias.

People have a hard time knowing whether the algorithm they use is 'fair'.

Furthermore, even the choice of model to use is also a difficult decision we know little about. All of this leads up to our research question:

are there some machine learning models that are more suited for fairness than others?
